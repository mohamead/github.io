Adrik has a master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Oslo (UIO). He also has a bachelor's degree in Dynamic Web-design from the University College of Southeast Norway (HSN). Adrik has through education gained great insight into IT and especially in programming, agile systems, database administration, usability and interaction design. In addition to his studies, he has for two years been employed by Schlumberger as a system developer where he programmed in Microsoft.NET and contributed to work related to optical sensors.

He now works at Sogeti as a test developer with expertise in testing and software development within the norwegian energy sector. He has participated in Sogeti's trainee program in autumn 2015, where he has undergone a 5-week intensive test course at nordic level. The program has given him broad knowledge of the test subject and prepared him to hold several different test positions. Adrik is passionate about working in situations where good problem-solving abilities are required. The combination of teamwork and independent work is especially something that appeals to him. He is positive, responsible, and fond of technical challenges.

Adrik is certified in TMAP NEXT® Test Engineer, ISTQB® Foundation, ISTQB® Agile Tester and HP Unified Functional Testing.
